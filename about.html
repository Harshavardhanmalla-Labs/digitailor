About DigiTailor

DigiTailor is an AI-powered resume and career tool designed to help job seekers craft tailored applications that stand out. Our platform offers resume analysis, tailoring, and LinkedIn optimization—all with a clean, modern experience.

Why DigiTailor?

We built DigiTailor because we believe everyone deserves a chance to present their best self—no expensive coaches or complicated tools needed. Whether you're a student or a professional, we offer powerful guidance for free.

Our mission is to democratize access to career tools using smart design and intelligent technology.
